Madras Medical Mission Hospital

The Madras Medical Mission (MMM) Hospital, located in Mogappair, Chennai, is one of India's premier healthcare institutions, renowned for its excellence in medical care and research. Established in 1987 as a non-profit charitable organization, the hospital has grown into a multi-specialty facility providing world-class healthcare services to patients from across the globe. The hospital was founded with a mission to combine compassion, quality care, and advanced medical technology. Over the years, it has become synonymous with excellence in medical treatment, particularly in the fields of cardiology, nephrology, and critical care.
